About me

Welcome to Automotive Heaven – where my passion for photography, incredible cars, and exploring the world come together.

So, who am I?
Hey, I’m Leonard Bonkas– a photographer, storyteller, and car enthusiast. Capturing moments that would otherwise slip away is what drives me. Photography isn’t just about taking pictures; it’s about creating something unforgettable!

When I think about what fuels my passion, a few things come to mind:
  • Freezing time – turning fleeting moments into lasting memories
  • Pushing creative boundaries – experimenting with new styles and perspectives
  • Seeing the world through frames – some I capture, some get away, but all inspire me
  • Meeting extraordinary people – from Bali’s scooter riders to Switzerland’s hypercar owners, every encounter adds a new story to my journey

Over the years, I’ve had the privilege of covering high-end events, private collections, and exclusive showrooms. Each project fuels my drive to create extraordinary content and share unique perspectives.

Ready to see the world through my lens?

Join me in discovering breathtaking landscapes, the rarest and most exclusive cars, and the events that bring passionate people together. This is more than photography – it’s about the experience, the thrill, and the stories behind every shot.

Let’s connect! Whether you’re looking for stunning visuals, event coverage, or a creative collaboration – reach out and let’s make something amazing together. And stay tuned – there’s much more to come!
